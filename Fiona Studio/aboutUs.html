ABOUT FIONA STUDIO

Established in the year 2021, Fiona Studio has its offices in Pune and Satara and operations spread across India. Fiona Studio has won many awards as the Best Interior designers in Pune and Best lnterior Designers in Satara. The ever expanding, multiple awards winning Interior Design Company is the brainchild of its founders and creative heads – Yogesh Lad and Rushikesh Lad. Between the two, it’s a heady mix between bold, beautiful and sophisticated designs that are pragmatic. Above all, they infuse warmth and character into the spaces that they design!

THE TEAM

It takes a village to realise a design dream! Combined with a vision to deliver designs par excellence while staying true to the essence of what the customer needs and the spatial requirements, the team behind Xclusive – designers, craftsmen, technicians and more – tirelessly works to breathe life into the spaces that we design. Currently holding a capacity to handle as many as 45 projects simultaneously, our highly creative and ingenious team dedicates themselves to every new project with equal amount of gusto and ensures that each new space designed delivers high in terms of customer-satisfaction levels. Our team is an extension of our grand vision behind each member is instrumental in contributing to all the awesome things that we do here!
